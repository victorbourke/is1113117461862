Favourite Country

I love my home-country Ireland. I think for such a small country we have produced so many world class athletes and intellectuals it is incredible. I love the lush green countryside and the friendliness of the people. We are also lucky to be steeped in history dating back many hundred of years.
Most Visited

The country I have most visited is Italy. Italy has a special place in my heart as I have been going there since I was a child. I love the incredible food and the slow way of life in the countryside.
Most want to Visit

The country I would most like to visit is America, mainly New York. I would love to see all the top end designer shops and the towering sky scrapers.
